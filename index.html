Welcome 👋

3D Sketch AI is an open-source 3D modeling tool exploring a mix of parametric structure and direct editing.

That openness is intentional. The aim is to remove friction from traditional CAD while keeping the system precise, powerful, and shaped in public.

Heads up

This is an early build. Things may break. Features may be incomplete or change as the system evolves.

Some operations may not be fully reversible yet, so avoid critical work.
